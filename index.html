How to Calculate EMI Manually (Step by Step)

  1. Take your total loan amount (Principal P).
  2. Convert annual interest rate to monthly: R = annualRate / 12 / 100.
  3. Convert tenure in years to months: N = years * 12 (or directly months).
  4. Apply formula: EMI = [P × R × (1 + R)^N] / [(1 + R)^N – 1].
  5. Use our calculator to automate this and get amortization with CSV export.
